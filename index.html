About Me

Hi! My name is Ross Murray and I study Web Design and Web Programming at Saint Paul College in St. Paul, Minnesota. I'm planning on graduating in December 2025 with a Computer Graphics & Visualization AS degree. This website serves as a portfolio of my skills developing and designing websites!

Currently, I'm taking the following classes at Saint Paul College:

  • Web Fundamentals, where I learned HTML & CSS!
  • Introduction to Computer Graphics, where I learned a variety of software to aid in designing websites!

Next semester, I'll be taking Graphical Web Design in order to acquire a better feel for designing websites. In addition to this, I'll also be taking Blogging Applications to better understand SEO and site traffic analytics.

In future, I'll also be taking Digital Sound and Video, and pursuing classes focusing on tools such as Photoshop and Adobe Animate. I'm confident that these will be invaluable to developing my skills as a web developer!

A few of my hobbies include video games, photography and graphic design!
